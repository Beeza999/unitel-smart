Support
Help & FAQ
How do I reserve a SIM number?
Click on any SIM number card, then click "Reserve Now". You'll need to be logged in or provide your contact details. The number will be reserved for 24 hours.
How long does SIM delivery take?
Home delivery takes 1-3 business days nationwide. Pick up at any Unitel store is ready within 1 hour. eSIM is delivered to email instantly.
How does the AI Package Finder work?
The AI asks you 4 questions about your usage habits, budget, and preferences. It then calculates a match score for each package and recommends the best 3 options for you.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Cash on Delivery, Bank Transfer (BCEL, LDB, APB), QR Payment via OnePay/BCEL One, and payment at any Unitel store.
Can I return a SIM number after purchase?
Reserved numbers can be cancelled within 24 hours. Once a SIM is activated, returns are not possible. Please contact our support team for special cases.
What devices support eSIM?
Most modern smartphones support eSIM including iPhone XS and newer, Samsung Galaxy S20+, Google Pixel 3+, and many more. Check your device settings for eSIM compatibility.
Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7
